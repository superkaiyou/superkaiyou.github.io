Journey of XXX

Journey of XXX is an interactive game that narrates the impact of the COVID-related cyberbullying on mental health of patients and survivors in their daily life. The goal of the project is to call attention to the impact of cyberbullying on the mental health of COVID patients and survivors and oppose COVID-related cyberbullying in China.
